Be Prepared

Comprehensive Document Checklist

  • Aadhaar Card: Mandatory for KYC.
  • PAN Card: Required for financial tracking.
  • Passport Size Photo: Recent color photographs.
  • Bank Statement: Last 6 to 12 months reflecting income.
  • Income Proof: Salary Slips / Form 16 / ITR.
  • Business Proof: GST Certificate / Trade License.
  • Udyam Registration: Specifically for MSME loans.
  • Property Papers: Title deed, chain documents (for LAP/Home Loan).
  • Vehicle RC & Insurance: For commercial or personal vehicle loans.
