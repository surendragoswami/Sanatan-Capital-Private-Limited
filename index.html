Be Prepared

Required Documents

  • KYC Documents: PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Passport/Voter ID.
  • Income Proof: Last 3 months Salary Slips, Form 16 (Salaried), 3 years ITR (Self-Employed).
  • Bank Statements: Last 6 months bank statement showing income/salary credit.
  • Business Proof: GST Registration, MSME Certificate (for Business Loans).
  • Property Papers: Chain of property documents (for LAP/Home Loans).
